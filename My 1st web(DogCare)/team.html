OUR TEAM

With over a decade of experience in animal care and management, Emily oversees the day-to-day operations of our facility. Her dedication to providing a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for both dogs and their owners ensures that every visit to our dog care home is a positive experience.

As our lead trainer, James has a passion for helping dogs reach their full potential through positive reinforcement and effective training techniques. With certifications in canine behavior and training, James works closely with both dogs and their owners to address behavioral issues, improve obedience, and strengthen the bond between human and canine.

As our customer care specialist, Emma is the friendly face you'll see when you first walk through our doors. With a warm smile and a passion for helping both dogs and their owners, Emma is dedicated to providing exceptional service and answering any questions you may have about our facility and services.
